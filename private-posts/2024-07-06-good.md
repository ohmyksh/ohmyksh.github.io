title: "On Being Good"
date: 2024-07-06
---

To be good at something is not just about skill or talent. It’s about being able to perform well in what I want and need to do, without being crushed by the weight of responsibility, pressure, and stress that surround it.

Adults often say that I’m still young, that this is the time to try anything I want (I partly agree). Yet, compared to a few years ago, I can now feel a heaviness I didn’t before — a sense of responsibility that quietly grows with time.

To truly focus on what I want to do, I need both the energy to pour myself into it and the mental strength to stay grounded even when things get hard.
